About

Sarah Bump is a full stack web developer hailing from Chicago, Illinois. She earned her degree from St. Norbert College and gained her web development skills from the Web Development Immersive program at General Assembly. Before General Assembly, she worked as a marketing professional at Assurance.

During her time at Assurance, she developed her project management skills and became interested in web development by creating the automation for both Assurance University and the Assurance Blog. Through an agile workflow, Sarah is excited to create meaningful and engaging applications.

Riots on Rails

"Riots on Rails" is a rails application that uses D3 Visualization to show race riots over time across the country. We used geoJSON to render the data and the dots expand based upon the number arrests at that riot. You can click on the dots to access more information on the riot. We worked as an agile team and together, collected the data from Wikipedia. For styling we used a framework called materialize. Click here to View the Github respository.

Volunteer Sign-Up

Volunteer Sign-Up is a sinatra application that allows volunteers to sign-up for events. It uses Bootstrap to render the volunteers who have signed up onto the admin logged-in page and the nexmo API to send volunteers a text message when they have signed up to volunteer. Click here to View the Github respository.

Chicago Farmers Market Finder
